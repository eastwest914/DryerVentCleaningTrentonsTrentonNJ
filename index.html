FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about air duct cleaning, dryer vent cleaning and our Trenton NJ services.

How often should I clean my air ducts?

The National Air Duct Cleaners Association (NADCA) recommends cleaning your air ducts every 3 to 5 years. However, if you have pets, allergies, recent renovations, or visible mold in your ducts, you may need more frequent cleaning. We offer a free inspection to help you determine the right schedule for your home.

How do I know if my dryer vent needs cleaning?

Warning signs include clothes taking longer than usual to dry, the dryer getting very hot to the touch, a burning smell during operation, or excessive lint around the dryer opening. If your dryer vent has not been cleaned in over a year, we strongly recommend scheduling a service to prevent fire hazards.

How long does air duct cleaning take?

A standard residential air duct cleaning typically takes between 2 to 4 hours depending on the size of your home and the number of vents. Commercial properties may take longer. We give you an accurate time estimate before we begin so you can plan your day accordingly.

Do you serve areas outside Trenton, NJ?

Yes, we serve Trenton and surrounding communities throughout Mercer County including Hamilton, Ewing, Lawrence, Princeton, and more. Call us at 609-728-0040 to confirm service availability in your area. We also handle commercial properties across central New Jersey.

Is air duct cleaning worth it?

Absolutely. Clean air ducts improve indoor air quality, reduce allergens and dust in your home, help your HVAC system run more efficiently, and can lower your energy bills. For households with allergy sufferers, asthma patients or young children, the health benefits alone make it a worthwhile investment every few years.

Dryer Vent Cleaning

A clogged dryer vent is one of the most common and preventable causes of house fires in the United States. Lint buildup restricts airflow, causing your dryer to overheat and potentially ignite. Our dryer vent cleaning service thoroughly removes all lint, debris and blockages from the vent line, from the dryer outlet all the way to the exterior exhaust point. We also inspect the vent for damage, kinks or improper installation that could be causing issues. After our service, your dryer runs faster, uses less energy and most importantly, your family is safe.
